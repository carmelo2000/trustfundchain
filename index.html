About Us

TRUSTFUNDCHAIN is a pioneering NGO that combines self-sovereign digital identity with total transparency in social fund distribution.

Our Mission

To empower individuals without documentation through blockchain technology while ensuring complete transparency in social fund distribution.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has access to digital identity and financial services, regardless of their documentation status.

Our Values

Transparency, Innovation, Inclusion, and Trust are the core values that drive everything we do.

Our Team

Composed of blockchain experts, social workers, and technology innovators committed to social change.
